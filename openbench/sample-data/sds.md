# Safety Data Sheet — Reagent A (Reference Standard, 1 g/L aqueous)

**Revision:** v2.1  **Issue date:** 2026-02-01

## 1. Identification

- Product name: Reagent A — Reference standard, 1 g/L aqueous solution
- Recommended use: Laboratory reference standard for HPLC calibration
- Manufacturer: Demo Lab Reagents, Inc.

## 2. Hazard identification

- GHS classification: **Not classified as hazardous** under standard handling conditions.
- Potential effects: Mild eye irritation on splash contact.

## 3. Composition / information on ingredients

- Aqueous solution; reference analyte at 1 g/L; no other regulated components.

## 4. First-aid measures

- **Eye contact:** Rinse with clean water for 15 minutes; seek medical advice if
  irritation persists.
- **Skin contact:** Wash with soap and water.
- **Ingestion:** Rinse mouth, drink water, seek medical advice if symptoms persist.
- **Inhalation:** Move to fresh air.

## 5. Fire-fighting measures

- Not flammable. Use extinguishing media appropriate for surrounding fire.

## 6. Accidental release

- Wipe up small spills with absorbent material; rinse area with water.
- Place absorbent material in the labeled aqueous waste container.

## 7. Handling and storage

- Store at 2–8 °C in original container.
- Keep container tightly closed when not in use.
- Avoid contamination with foreign substances.

## 8. Exposure controls / personal protection

- Engineering controls: bench-top use in a well-ventilated area is sufficient.
- PPE:
  - **Eye protection:** ANSI Z87.1 safety glasses or splash goggles.
  - **Hand protection:** Nitrile gloves.
  - **Body protection:** Lab coat.

## 9. Stability and reactivity

- Stable under normal conditions.
- Avoid contact with strong oxidizers.

## 10. Disposal

- Dispose of in accordance with institutional aqueous-waste guidelines.

## 11. Transport / regulatory

- Not regulated for transport.
