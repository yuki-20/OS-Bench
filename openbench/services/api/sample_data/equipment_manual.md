# BP-1000 Pipette — Operator Manual (Excerpt)

**Model:** BP-1000  **Volume range:** 100–1000 µL  **Last calibration:** see calibration log

## 1. Overview

The BP-1000 is a single-channel air-displacement pipette. Operate only with disposable
polypropylene tips rated for 1000 µL.

## 2. Pre-use checks

Before use, verify:

- The volume display is **powered on** and shows the requested volume.
- The pipette is held vertically when aspirating.
- Calibration sticker on the body is current (within 12 months).

If the display does not power on, do not use the pipette; tag it and inform the lab manager.

## 3. Operation

- Press the button to the first stop to aspirate.
- Press to the second stop to dispense.
- Always use a new tip for each tube to avoid cross-contamination.

## 4. Cleaning

- Wipe the body with 70% IPA after each session.
- Do not autoclave.

## 5. Error states

- Display shows `E1`: pipette overpressured. Turn off and contact maintenance.
- Display shows `E2`: low battery. Replace as instructed in section 7.

## 6. Maintenance

- Annual factory calibration required. Refer to the calibration log.

## 7. Battery replacement

- Replace with two AAA alkaline batteries.

## 8. Storage

- Hang upright on the bench rack with the tip pointing down.
